# Reference Material Used in LotS

The Laws of the Spirit (LotS) project draws on a wide range of reference material organized across the other sections of this volume:

- **[Scholarly and Academic Sources](scholarly-academic-sources.md)** — Peer-reviewed and academic works from fields including theology, education, psychology, and physics
- **[Spiritual Formation and Theological Authors](spiritual-formation-authors.md)** — Authors whose work on spiritual formation, inner healing, and the life of the Spirit has directly shaped the explorations
- **[Processes and Ministry Tools](processes-and-ministry-tools.md)** — Specific methodologies and tools tested in practice, including the Grief Recovery Handbook and Transformation Prayer Ministry
- **[Scripture Figures as Case Studies](scripture-case-studies.md)** — Biblical figures examined as test cases for the proposed laws
- **[Project Participants](project-participants.md)** — Members of the workshop community who contributed through shared practice and feedback

The **[Formation Documents](../formation-documents/)** — companion papers on heart formation, soul and spirit taxonomies, and a model for spiritual formation — serve as both reference material and extensions of the investigation.
