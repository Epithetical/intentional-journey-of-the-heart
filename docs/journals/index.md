# Journals and Devotionals

This section will host John's personal journal entries, devotional writings, and reflections as the project grows.

John has been journaling since shortly after coming to the Lord in 1982. These entries trace the footprints of God through decades of personal experience, forming the raw material from which many of the explorations in the five volumes emerged.

*New entries will appear here as they are added.*
