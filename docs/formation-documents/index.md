# Formation Documents

Three companion papers grew alongside the five volumes as part of the same exploration. They are significant enough to warrant their own section.

## [The Heart in Formation](heart-in-formation.md)
Applies the affective taxonomy (Bloom's taxonomy adapted for spiritual formation) as a framework for heart training. Explores what spiritual formation is actually measuring and why trust in scripture is the right test case.

## [Soul and Spirit Taxonomies](soul-spirit-taxonomies.md)
Develops two parallel taxonomies — one for the soul (from natural life to Spirit-governed integration) and one for the spirit (from regeneration to union) — and maps them as an interdependent system with the heart taxonomy.

## [A Model of Spiritual Formation for Individuals and Small Groups](model-spiritual-formation.md)
A comprehensive paper with abstract, theological background (Old Testament, New Testament, Pauline theology), the central argument applying the affective taxonomy, testing the model through preaching and worship, and integrating challenges including suffering as a formation engine.
