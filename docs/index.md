# Intentional Journey of the Heart

*Notes to My Kids (and Grandkids): On My Personal Exploration of the Laws of God's Love*

**By John G. Tittle** | March 2026, Revised Edition

---

I have been "nudged" by the Spirit to get my thoughts out in a way that others can understand on this subject. What if the way the world of the Spirit works is just as logical and discoverable as the physical world? That question has driven this exploration for over two decades.

Jesus rebuked Nicodemus not for a lack of piety but for being a teacher in Israel who did not understand how the spiritual world operated. The goal here is not to replace the direct experience of the Spirit, but to map the territory — to discover the laws and relationships that govern spiritual life the way physics maps the natural world.

## The Volumes

**[Introduction](introduction/)** — What I am trying to do, the four foundational principles, and an overview of the entire work.

**[Volume 1: Laws of the Spirit](volume-1-laws-of-the-spirit/)** — Eight core explorations covering faith, the structure of spirit/heart/soul/body, the relationship of faith/hope/love, spiritual authority, prayer as resonance, and more. Includes a proposed "periodic table" of spiritual laws.

**[Volume 2: Knowing to Doing](volume-2-knowing-to-doing/)** — The bridge from understanding to practice. Diagnostic tools for the heart, emotional blockage clearing (grief, shame, fear, anger knots), the tool map, hearing with understanding, community as amplifier, and a training plan.

**[Volume 3: Quantitative Framework](volume-3-quantitative-framework/)** — An ambitious attempt to model spiritual dynamics quantitatively using field theory, systems dynamics, and conservation laws. Includes the Master Law Index.

**[Volume 4: Testing Framework](volume-4-testing-framework/)** — Epistemological foundations and individual spiritual formation testing protocols. How would you test these claims?

**[Volume 5: References](volume-5-references/)** — Annotated bibliography covering scholarly sources, theological authors, ministry tools, and scripture case studies.

**[Formation Documents](formation-documents/)** — Three companion papers on heart formation, soul and spirit taxonomies, and a model of spiritual formation.
