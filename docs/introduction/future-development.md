# Future Development

Speaking of future explorations and relative certainty, all of Vol 3 (Spiritual Dynamics) is open for challenge. Transcendental Field Theory (TFT) needs someone with the right background in Quantum Field Theory math and who is also scripturally grounded to review and test the concepts and implications. Vol 2 is a collection of heart work processes that I and others have tested over many years, and that can be very effective. This was actually where my journey started, at the small-group process level. It was later that the connection with the laws of the Spirit was made. Vol 1 contains the current draft of the Laws of the Spirit. Most are solid in my opinion, but I am clear about what needs further work. Also, the Periodic Table of Elements in Vol 1, which arranges the laws to look for gaps, is notional and needs testing. One easy test is to see whether the table in fact predicts a new law I had not seen before, but that is there in scripture.

I expect to continue developing this document of discovery as long as I have breath. And when that is done, I’ll get to see how much is true.  I’m sure that much of what I am offering here is gold, silver, and precious stones, but some is also wood, hay, and stubble. I just don’t know for sure which is which at this point, but I will know when I stand face to face with my Lord.

In all this, I am inviting you into an intentional journey of the heart. A journey needs a goal, and in this case, that is into the heart of God, who invites us to work with him in deeper and deeper fellowship and oneness. Scripture describes such a life, and we can respond. I’m on such a journey, and this document captures at least some of that journey. However, you will have your own journey, and that journey will be as individual as your fingerprints. 

Please join me in this intentional journey. As C.S. Lewis said in “The Last Battle”, “Further up and further in.” I’ll meet you there.
