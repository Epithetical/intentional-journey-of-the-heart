## About This Work

What if the way the world of the Spirit works is just as logical and discoverable
as the physical world? This multi-volume theological exploration investigates the
laws and relationships governing spiritual life, drawing from scripture, personal
experience, scholarly research, and decades of journaling and community practice.

## The Five Volumes

- **Volume 1 — Laws of the Spirit**: Core explorations of faith, prayer, spiritual
  authority, and the structural relationships that govern spiritual dynamics
- **Volume 2 — Knowing to Doing**: The bridge from understanding to practice,
  including diagnostic tools, emotional blockage clearing, and training plans
- **Volume 3 — Quantitative Framework**: An ambitious attempt to apply field theory,
  systems dynamics, and quantitative modeling to spiritual phenomena
- **Volume 4 — Testing Framework**: Epistemological foundations and protocols for
  testing the claims made across all volumes
- **Volume 5 — References**: Annotated bibliography of scholarly, theological, and
  ministry sources, plus scripture case studies

## Companion Documents

Three **Formation Documents** grew alongside the volumes as companion papers on
heart formation, soul and spirit taxonomies, and a model of spiritual formation
for individuals and small groups.
