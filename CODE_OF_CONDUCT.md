# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project exists to explore questions about the spiritual life with honesty,
rigor, and humility. We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful
environment for everyone, regardless of denominational background, theological
tradition, level of education, or stage of spiritual journey.

## Expected Behavior

- Engage with ideas charitably and in good faith
- Disagree with arguments, not people
- Cite scripture and sources when making claims
- Acknowledge uncertainty honestly
- Respect the author's original work and intent
- Welcome newcomers and answer questions patiently

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or dismissive language
- Denominational gatekeeping or claims of exclusive authority
- Harassment of any kind
- Deliberately misleading theological claims
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comment removal, temporary bans, or permanent removal
from the project at the discretion of the maintainers. Report concerns via
GitHub Issues or by contacting the maintainers directly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant v2.1.
